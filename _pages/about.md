I am Graduate Student at the [University of Houston-Clear Lake](https://www.uhcl.edu/). I earned my Bachelor's in Psychology from [University of Houston]([https://www.uh.edu/]). 

My research interests include machine learning, health statistics, and business analytics. I’m particularly focused on applying statistical modeling, machine learning, and data analysis to improve health outcomes and support evidence-based solutions in humanitarian and global health contexts

Through my academic and project work, I aim to translate complex data into meaningful insights that can help organizations make informed decisions, strengthen health systems, and better serve vulnerable communities.
